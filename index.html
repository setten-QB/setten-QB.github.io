Hiroaki Tanaka¶

I’m at Urban Sensing Research Group, Research Laboratories, NTT DOCOMO, Inc. from April 2018. I had been a master student in an Augmented Human Communication Laboratory at Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST) from April 2016 to March 2018. I received my B.S. degree in science and engineering from Shimane University in 2016 and M.E. degree in computer science in 2018 from NAIST.

I’m interested in the following topics:

  • application of data science (e.g. machine learning, statistics) to solving problems in the real world;
  • machine learning, the theory of it;
  • statistics, mathematical statistics.
